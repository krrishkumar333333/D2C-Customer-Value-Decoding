Key Analytical Findings
Loyalty Definition: Revealed-Preference (Definition A) validated with −0.395 promo-dependency correlation vs. +0.021 for frequency-only Definition B. Spend discipline, not purchase frequency, predicts genuine loyalty.
Geography: Arizona (29.2%), Alaska (29.2%), and Pennsylvania (27.0%) show the highest True Loyalist concentration — below-average promo sensitivity signals organic demand not yet deliberately targeted.
Category Risk: Outerwear carries the highest Bargain Hunter concentration (29.6%), followed by Accessories (28.3%) — both categories are most exposed to margin erosion from promotional cycles.
Strategic Recommendations
Promotional Sunset Plan
Phase out discounts for Bargain Hunters in Outerwear/Accessories over 2 quarters; protect Standard Customers with loyalty tiers instead.
Introduce subscription-gated early access as a discount substitute — 27% of the base is already subscribed.
Track: promo redemption rate, segment revenue share, and gross margin per category monthly.
Ideal Customer Acquisition Profile
Ages 30–44, Express shipping preference, non-promo buyer, subscription enrolled.
Geo-target Arizona, Alaska, and Pennsylvania with full-price brand campaigns.
Use Credit Card / PayPal payment affinity as a paid-media targeting signal.
